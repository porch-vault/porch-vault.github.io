About Us: Porch Vault Team

The Team

The team consists of members: Bennett Fleming, Justin Borromeo, Matthew Romlewski, and Sean O’Sullivan.

Mission

Over the past decade, the popularity of e-commerce has exploded with consumers attracted to the convenience of ordering via the Internet and having goods delivered in less than a week. With most of these packages being delivered to residences, porch piracy has become an increasingly prevalent problem. Every day in the US, ~$25M of delivered goods are reported as stolen. Most existing solutions are either expensive or involve inconveniencing consumers. We are working on the Porch Vault, a device that prevents porch piracy while maintaining the existing convenience and cost-effectiveness of e-commerce.
